# Steering Committee
STRUDEL has a steering committee that is organized according to [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md). See below for current members and its roadmap. 

**Steering Committee members**  
Lavanya Ramakrishnan, Lawrence Berkeley National Lab, UC Berkeley  
Drew Paine, Lawrence Berkeley National Lab, UC Berkeley  

**Steering Committee Roadmap:**   
*Current priorities:*  
* Help establish and refine governance models and contribution guides for each STRUDEL working group  
* Update strudel.science to include governance resources

*Near future priorities:*  
* Re-assess STRUDEL governance structures after funding from the Sloan Foundation concludes

*Long term priorities:*  
* Deliver a suite of products that demonstrably increases UX capacity in research software teams

\
